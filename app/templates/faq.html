The Network of Innovators project, in addition to giving individuals the ability to articulate and search for more specific expertise within topic areas, defines expertise more broadly and aims to make skills, experiences, and know how searchable.") }}

  • {{ gettext("Why do I need to answer questions about what I know?") }}

    {{ gettext("NoI is premised on the belief that simple tags (e.g., my skills are “open data” and “crowdsourcing”) do not adequately capture the full gamut of experience and know how possessed by governance innovators. To help move beyond free-form tags, NoI asks you to note if and how you could answer a specific innovation question from someone looking to access your expertise. This approach, we believe, will help you to better describe the range and nuance of your innovation expertise, and enable more fruitful connections with others on NoI.") }}

  • {{ gettext("How does your matching algorithm work?") }}

    {{ gettext("NOI uses the answers you give to the innovation skill questions to match you with others from whom you can learn the most. Based on all innovation skills that you answer “I want to learn”, NOI’s algorithm ranks the innovators in the network who can do, explain or connect you on most of them. This is also how others who can learn the most from your expertise will be able to find and consult with you.") }}
