Network of Innovators (NoI) is an experimental platform designed and managed by the GovLab to identify people and search for expertise across organizations, institutions, and communities. NHS England is collaborating with the GovLab to test this platform at the NHS Expo to enable conference attendees to test their knowledge of innovation practices and be matched with others who share similar interests and skills.
